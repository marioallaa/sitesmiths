FAQ

How long will it take to complete my website?

The timeline for website completion varies based on complexity and client requirements. 
A simple website might take 2-4 weeks, while a more complex site, especially a restaurant/bar website or those requiring custom features, can take several months. 

We'll provide a more accurate timeline after discussing your specific needs.

What's the difference between a custom web design and a template-based design?

A custom web design is tailored specifically to your brand and unique needs, ensuring a one-of-a-kind online presence.  It offers flexibility in terms of design and functionality. 
On the other hand, a template-based design uses pre-made layouts which can be customized to some extent.

While templates can be quicker and more cost-effective, they might not offer the same level of uniqueness as a custom design.

Will my website be mobile-friendly?

Yes, all our websites are designed to be responsive, ensuring they look and function well on various devices, including desktops, tablets, and smartphones. Mobile-friendliness is crucial for user experience and SEO.

How do you handle website maintenance and updates after launch?

We offer ongoing maintenance packages that cover regular updates, security checks, backups, and any necessary adjustments to ensure your website's optimal performance. Depending on your needs, you can choose monthly, quarterly, or yearly maintenance plans.
